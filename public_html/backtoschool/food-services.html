For the 2025–2026 school year, breakfast and lunch are free for all students because the Clark County School District participates in the Community Eligibility Provision of the federal Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act.

The Food Service website, ccsd.net/foodservice, provides CCSD's nutrition policy as well as the approved snack and beverage list. The site also links to the digital platform where the school menus and nutrition details can be found:menu.ccsd.net.

Visit myschoolbucks.com to add money to a student’s meal account for á la carte purchases and view what items your child has purchased. Anyone who needs assistance or has questions may call 1-800-819-7556 or email myschoolmeals@nv.ccsd.net.

At the beginning of the school year, please review your student’s special diet with the school’s Health Office. Click here to find the forms to renew, update, or discontinue a special diet.

This institution is an equal opportunity provider.

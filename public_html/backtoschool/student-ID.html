Student ID

During the 2024-2025 school year there is a new item you should make sure your child has every morning. Beginning this year all middle school and high school students will be required to wear a student ID on campus.

Students will be asked to wear their student ID’s on a breakaway lanyard provided by the school. The purpose of the change is to ensure staff can identify all students.

Additionally, the student ID’s will carry important information about SafeVoice and other programs in compliance with Nevada law.

Should your child lose their student ID, please contact the school’s main office for information about a replacement as soon as possible.

We thank all our families for working together to keep CCSD campuses safe.
